# Move Able MVP

## **Project Overview** {#project-overview}

**Primary Goal**

To develop a Progressive Web Application for Total Therapy to assist physiotherapists and patients with keeping track of therapy progress, and recovery.

**Objectives**

* Streamline communication and exercise management between physiotherapists and patients.

* Implement a secure, role-based platform for doctors to track patient recovery progress and exercise adherence.

* Gamify the patient recovery experience to increase engagement, compliance, and motivation.


**Key Outcomes and Benefits**

* **Improved Recovery Tracking:** Physiotherapists can monitor patient progress through integrated exercise and pain scale tracking, enabling data-driven adjustments to treatment plans.

* **Enhanced Patient Engagement:** Gamified features, such as daily streaks and progress bars, encourage patients to maintain their exercise routines and stay committed to their recovery.

* **Efficient Care Coordination:** A centralized messaging platform and streamlined video sharing system facilitate seamless communication and clarity between care teams and patients.

* **Scalable Management:** The platform supports multi-user management, allowing physiotherapists to invite patients, assign tailored exercises, and view average performance statistics efficiently.

## **Scope of Work** 

The following is a detailed outline of the work to be completed under this proposal.

* Role based access for Care Providers and Patients  
* Gamification:  
  * Daily exercise streaks  
* Video recording and assignment  
  * Videos are recorded of a patient, and only shared with that patient.  
  * There are two types of videos:  
    * Exercise Guidance  
    * Progress tracking (For example, seeing range of motion)  
  * Video player must include speed controls.  
* Exercise creation and assignment  
  * Exercises assigned to a patient are given as sessions.  
  * Sessions reset daily, and patients are encouraged to complete the entire session.  
  * The daily streak will be incremented once a session is completed.  
  * Exercises are connected with videos  
  * Exercises include \# of sets and reps to complete, along with the name of the exercise  
* Care Provider capabilities:  
  * Invite patients to join with a one time code, and remove them.  
  * Record videos of patients and share with them as exercises.  
  * Create, view, replace and delete videos and exercises for a patient.  
  * View their list of patients, and statistics about each patient.  
  * View the average statistics of their patients.  
  * Assign notes to patients for the doctor’s reference.  
  * Export patient statistics as a PDF.  
* Patient capabilities:  
  * Use an invite code to join under a Provider. (Currently, a patient may only have one provider).  
  * View exercises assigned to them, and the related videos.  
  * Change the order of exercises in a session, and begin the session  
  * Mark a session as complete, and provide feedback
